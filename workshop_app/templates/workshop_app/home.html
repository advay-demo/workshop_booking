Welcome to FOSSEE Workshops

Discover a learning experience designed to go beyond traditional classrooms. FOSSEE Workshops bring together students, educators, and industry experts to collaborate, build, and innovate using powerful open-source tools.

Whether you are just starting your journey or looking to deepen your technical expertise, our hands-on workshops provide real-world exposure through guided sessions, practical problem-solving, and interactive learning environments. Each workshop is carefully structured to help you gain not just knowledge, but confidence in applying it.

From programming and simulation to scientific computing and engineering tools, you will have the opportunity to explore a wide range of domains while learning directly from experienced instructors. Join a growing community of learners who are building skills that matter.

Start your journey today — explore workshops, connect with mentors, and take your first step towards mastering open-source technologies.
